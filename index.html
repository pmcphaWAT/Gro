ABOUT GRO - THE MISSION.

At a local level, growers seek to reduce their carbon footprint, promote access to fresh food and connect with their neighbors. Greater still is a lack of access to affordable healthy food that many American’s face. Gro seeks to bridge the gap between local growers and their neighbors in order to share healthy food with those in need, reduce food waste, and promote community GROwth. Do you have a garden with extra produce that you would like to share with your neighbors? Add your garden to the map!
